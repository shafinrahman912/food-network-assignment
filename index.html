Flap Steak

Flap steak, or flap meat is a low-end beef steak cut. It comes from a bottom sirloin butt cut of beef, and is generally a very thin steak. Flap steak is sometimes called sirloin tips in New England, but is typically ground for hamburger or sausage meat, elsewhere.
